Budget Travel Tips for India

Accommodation

Book in advance, especially during peak season. Look for budget hotels, guesthouses, and hostels. OYO and similar platforms offer great deals.

Transportation

Use Indian Railways for long distances - book AC classes for comfort. For short trips, consider state transport buses or ride-sharing apps.

Food

Eat at local dhabas and street food stalls for authentic and affordable meals. Thali meals at restaurants offer great value.

Attractions

Many monuments offer free entry on certain days. E-tickets often have discounts. Student and senior citizen concessions are available.

Timing

Travel during shoulder season (April-May, September-October) for better deals. Avoid peak festival times for lower costs.

Money

Use ATMs from reputable banks. Keep some cash for smaller towns. UPI payments are widely accepted in cities.
